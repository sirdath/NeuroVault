# NeuroVault Trademark Guidelines

The NeuroVault name, official application icon, and distinctive visual
identity identify the official NeuroVault project and commercial Desktop app.
Source-code licenses do not grant trademark rights.

Truthful references such as "compatible with NeuroVault Core" and "forked
from NeuroVault Core" are welcome. Redistributed or substantially modified
applications should use a different product name and visual identity and must
not imply official status or endorsement without written permission.
